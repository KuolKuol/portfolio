Kilimanjaro Contracting

/ˌkɪlɪmənˈdʒɑːroʊ kənˈtræktiNG/

Summary

When I started working with Kilimanjaro Contracting they were struggling to obtain a professional digital presence. All of their marketing was done through word of mouth and from the ads on their trucks. The owner was looking for an eye catching and informative website that accurately represents them online. I followed their brand guidelines and created a website that captured the brands mission.


What I Learned

  • How to follow brand guidlines when developing a website.
  • How to explain complex concepts simply to non-techies.
