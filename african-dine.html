African Dine

/ˈafrəkən dīn/

Summary

African Dine is an old and established restaurant with a new owner that wanted to keep the existing customer base of the former owner while finding new ways to attract new visitors. In order for me to help them retain former customers and gain new ones I participated in a rebranding strategy that resulted in a new website, logo, street sign and menu design.
