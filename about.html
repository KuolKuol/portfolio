Meet The Kuol

I started my web development journey when I got my first laptop at the age of 14. While browsing the web trying to find ways of how to develop a video games I stumbled upon website development tutorials on codecademy

After graduating from high school I took a year off to decide what I wanted to do with my life. During that time, I decided to start building websites for local businesses with the web development knowledge I accumulated through the years of completing online tutorials.

After a year of freelancing I decided that I wanted to take my skills to the next level so I signed up for the SAIT web developer certificate program.

After attending SAIT I eventually got hired by Bill Gates to takeover Microsoft.
